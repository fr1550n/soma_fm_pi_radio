GFX HAT

GFX HAT uses both SPI and I2c to drive the LCD display, backlight and touch. However both of these busses can be shared with other devices.

To get the HAT set up and ready to go you can use the one-line product installer:

  1. curl -sS https://get.pimoroni.com/gfxhat | bash

And follow the instructions!

Details

  • Made by Pimoroni
  • HAT form-factor
  • EEPROM product ID
  • Needs 5v and 3v3 power
  • Uses 7 GPIO pins
  • Communication over SPI
  • Communication over I2C
  • 0x2c: CAP1166
  • 0x54: SN3218
